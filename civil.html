Civil Engineering

The Department of Civil Engineering of Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore was established in the year 2012 with an annual intake of 60. The department has highly qualified and dedicated faculty members specialized in various areas of Civil Engineering. The vision of the department is to develop the Technical skills and knowledge of Civil Engineers to improve the infrastructure and economic welfare of the society. The Mission of the department is to provide value based education to meet the requirement of the society and to promote consultancy activities with the help of different orangnizations.
