Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established along with the inception of the institution in 2001. And the Post Graduate course was approved in the year of 2007.

The department of CSE is an approved Research Centre by Anna University of Technology, Chennai. It got R&D approval since 2007. More than 20 Research Scholars are doing their research activities under the Department. At presently the department has more than 600 students and 28 faculty members. With the Motto of “Empowering the Engineering Community”, the department is striving hard to produce the exceptional engineering society. The department provides an outstanding learning environment with the aid of qualified and dedicated faculties. The Department has 4 Doctorates and 7 Faculty members pursuing their Ph.D. in the field Computer Science and Engineering. The department has 28 experienced faculty members among them 4 are professors, 5 are Senior Assistant Professors and 19 Assistant Professors. The department is fully equipped with all hardware and software to cater the academic needs .Every year the department conducts minimum eight activities which consists of seminars, workshops, conferences and value added programmes for faculties as well as students.
