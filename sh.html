Science and Humanities

The Department of Science and Humanities came into existence since the inception of CIET in 2001. The department includes English, Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry. The objective of the department is to offer high quality education to students with the support of well-qualified, dedicated and highly motivated faculty members. The department has all necessary laboratory amenities for imparting knowledge to the students.
